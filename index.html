How It Works

Experience tournament golf in a fair and competitive environment, no matter your skill level.

1. Purchase a Hat

Buy an event-specific hat from our shop to secure your entry into a regional tournament.

2. Compete in Flights

Participate in flighted tournaments that ensure you compete against players of similar skill level.

3. Enjoy the Festival

Your hat serves as your ticket to the Beer and Wine festival following each tournament.
